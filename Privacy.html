Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. Learn how we protect your data in our anonymous Nigerian women's community.

Please read this Notice carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your personal information. By using or registering with our Services, you acknowledge that your personal information will be collected, used, processed and disclosed as outlined in this Privacy Notice.

Nigerian residents can find more information about their specific privacy rights in the Additional Information sections below.

1. Personal Information We Collect and How We Collect It

"Personal information" means information that identifies, relates to, describes, or could reasonably be linked with you. It does not include publicly available, deidentified, or aggregated information.

We and our vendors collect personal information:

  • Directly from you when you provide it
  • Automatically when you use the Services
  • From the Spill app (e.g., selfie verification)
  • From your contacts if they refer you
  • From vendors like Apple, Facebook, and Google during signup

A. Information You Provide

When signing up, we may request your:

  • Email address, date of birth, university, and location within Nigeria
  • Photograph (selfie) for verification
  • Relationship status or other profile fields
  • Messages, posts, and comments (text, images, audio)

B. Information Collected Automatically

We and our vendors use cookies, SDKs, and similar tools to gather:

  • Device identifiers, IP address, browser, OS, and usage data
  • Geolocation (when enabled)
  • Logs of activity, errors, and login attempts

C. Information Collected via the Spill App

The app may request permission to access:

  • Camera (mandatory selfie for verification)
  • Microphone (audio posts/comments)
  • Contacts (invite friends, only with consent)
  • Photos (uploading images)
  • Face ID (optional local device authentication)
  • Push Notifications (optional)

Interactive Features: Spill offers anonymous interactive features, including red/green flag voting, comments, and AI-powered chat guidance. These features may capture and store chat transcripts, reactions, and votes.

2. Use of Personal Information We Collect

We use your information to:

  • Verify your identity (selfie verification)
  • Enable posting, comments, reactions, and flag voting
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Personalize your feed (e.g., based on university or age range)
  • Send account-related notifications
  • Improve and secure Services (fraud detection, abuse prevention)
  • Conduct analytics and reporting
  • Operate business functions, legal compliance, and risk management

3. How We Disclose Personal Information We Collect

We may disclose your information:

  • To vendors (e.g., hosting, analytics, Cloudinary for image uploads)
  • As part of a business transfer (sale, merger, financing)
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce policies
  • To protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud
  • In emergencies
  • Internally to those who need it to provide Services

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or needed for business/legal purposes. You may request deletion through the app or by contacting us directly.

5. Security of Your Personal Information

We implement reasonable safeguards including end-to-end encryption for your data, but no system is 100% secure. Use strong, unique passwords and do not share them with anyone.

6. Additional Information for Nigerian Residents

We are committed to protecting the privacy rights of Nigerian users in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and other applicable laws.

Your Rights: Nigerian residents have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

We may share identifiers, usage data, and geolocation with analytics providers for service improvement purposes.

We do not knowingly collect or share data of users under 18.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: orderingpau@gmail.com

7. Your Choices

  • Delete Account: Available in app settings
  • Update Info: Within profile or by contacting us
  • Permissions: Control camera, contacts, microphone, push notifications in device/app settings
  • Communication Preferences: Opt out of marketing communications at any time

Uninstalling the app stops new data collection but doesn't delete your account.

8. Third-Party Disclosure and Links

Links to outside sites (e.g., background checks, registries) are governed by their own policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

9. Notice Concerning Minors

Spill is 18+ only. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

10. Additional Information About the Spill App

  • Age Restrictions: 18+ only
  • Content Moderation: Automated + human review for red flag/green flag posts, abusive content, etc.
  • User Reporting: Built-in reporting tools; violations may result in bans or law enforcement referrals
  • Anonymous Features: All posts and interactions are anonymous to protect user privacy
  • Verification: Selfie verification required to maintain community safety

11. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred outside Nigeria for processing and storage. We ensure safeguards consistent with this Notice and applicable Nigerian data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Notice from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the effective date and notify you through the app or email if the changes are material.
