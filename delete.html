Delete Your Account

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you would like to permanently delete your account and all associated data, follow the steps below.

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

📧
Contact Us to Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

📧 orderingpau@gmail.com

📋
What to Include in Your Request

When you email us, please provide the following information:

  • 1
    Email Address
    The email address you used to register your account
  • 2
    Full Name or Username
    Your full name or username (if applicable)
  • 3
    Deletion Statement
    "I request my account and all associated data to be permanently deleted."
  • 4
    Profile Screenshot (Optional)
    You may attach a screenshot of your logged-in profile to help us confirm your request faster

⚡
What Happens Next

Our Process:

✓
We will verify your identity using the email address provided
✓
Once verified, we will permanently delete your account and all associated data
✓
You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

🗂️
Data Handling

🗑️ Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile and account details
  • Posts, comments, and replies you've created
  • Any uploaded media (images, videos)
  • Personal preferences and settings
  • Chat history and messages

📝 Data That May Be Retained

  • Transaction or audit logs (legal compliance)
  • Anonymized analytics data
  • Data required for security purposes

These records are not linked to your active account once deleted.

Need Help or Have Questions?

If you're experiencing issues or just need support, we're here to help before you decide to delete your account.
